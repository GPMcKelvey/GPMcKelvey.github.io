About Me


Hi! I'm Garrett McKelvey. I'm a web developer in training with a diverse background & skill set, eventually looking for a front-end position. Currently I am working through the Eleven Fifty Web Development bootcamp. I also handle social media & events for a craft beer bar, The Sinkhole, in Bloomington. I do a little freelance photography and graphic design under the name Oso Vista. The photo in the header above is one I took at Goeth-Link Observatory in Moorseville, IN. Daffodils might just be my favorite flower.

I'm always looking for a creative outlet, from photography to woodworking, I enjoy design and building things. That's why coding is for me. I enjoy watching a project come to life and web development gives me the opportunity to design and build. I'm always looking for ways to improve things and to make them more intuitive. My passion is creating and it's so rewarding to be on a career path that will allow me to bring my passion to the table.
